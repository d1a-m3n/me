Payment Privacy and Transparency

Tesla ensures complete privacy and transparency in all payment processes. Customer payment information is treated with strict confidentiality and is never shared with unauthorized parties. Upfront Payment and Delivery Terms

To initiate the purchase of a Tesla vehicle, customers are required to make an upfront payment of sixty percent (60%) of the total vehicle cost in Dogecoin. Upon confirmation of this payment, Tesla will commence the delivery process. The remaining balance must be paid in full before the vehicle is officially handed over to the customer. All payments must be confirmed on the blockchain before any delivery arrangement is finalized.

Payment Confirmation and Receipt

Once a payment is received and confirmed, an electronic receipt will be issued to the customer’s registered email. This receipt will serve as official proof of payment and should be retained for record purposes. Tesla reserves the right to verify the authenticity of all cryptocurrency transactions to prevent fraud or payment disputes.
